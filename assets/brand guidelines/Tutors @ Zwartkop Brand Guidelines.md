# Tutors @ Zwartkop Brand Guidelines

---

## 1. Mission & Vision

* **Mission Statement**: To provide a calm, structured, and encouraging learning environment where every learner is understood and supported as an individual.

* **Commitment**: Committed to offering personalised academic guidance, building confidence, and nurturing emotional well-being through small classes and engaging experiences.

* **Scope**: Walking alongside learners from Grade RR to Grade 12 to help them overcome challenges and achieve success in school and life.

* **Vision**: To be a trusted learning home where every child feels safe, valued, and inspired to grow into a confident, capable learner.

* **Long-term Goal**: Each learner reaching their full potential through gentle guidance and a lifelong love for learning.

---

## 2. Logos & Usage

The brand utilizes four logo variations based on size and priority:

| Priority | Logo Type | Usage Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Priority 1** | Primary Logo (with slogan) | The main go-to logo for standard use.

 |
| **Priority 2** | Primary Logo (no slogan) | Second go-to if the slogan version cannot be used.

 |
| **Priority 3** | Secondary Logo | The third option if size constraints prevent primary use.

 |
| **Priority 4** | Tertiary Logo (Icon) | Lowest priority; used for the smallest sizes.

 |

### Core Logo Rules:

* **Proportions**: Do not stretch, distort, or change proportions.

* **Color**: Use only main brand colours.

* **Contrast**: Always ensure high contrast between the logo and other elements.

* **Orientation**: Do not rotate; always keep the logo horizontal.

* **Backgrounds**: Do not use on busy or image-heavy backgrounds.

* **Clear Space**: Always allow for sufficient clear space around the logo.

## 4. Typography

The brand uses two distinct typefaces to maintain a balance between decorative and readable text.

### **Blogh** (Heading Font)

* **Usage**: Only for large headings and within the main logo.

* **Decorative**: Can be used as a decorative element for brand assets.

### **Quicksand** (Body & Subheading Font)

* **Usage**: The main typeface for body copy, subheadings, and some titles.

* **Priority**: Should be used more frequently than "Blogh".

* **Readability**: Ensure high contrast between text and background elements.

---

## 5. Graphic Assets & Photography

* **Style**: Assets should be hand-drawn to convey a playful, down-to-earth feel.

* **Elements**: Play with hand-drawn shapes, illustrations, or logo elements like leaves.

* **Consistency**: Assets must stick to brand colours to maintain cohesiveness.

* **Photography**: Use natural light to showcase children's natural selves; no heavy photo treatment/filtering is allowed.

---

## 6. Brand Voice & Social Media

### **Brand Voice**

* **Tone**: Warm, playful, respectful, and inviting.

* **Persona**: Sounds like a caring mentor and cheerful guide—never strict or distant.

* **Keywords**: Encouraging, Playful (but purposeful), Respectful, Clear, and Inclusive.

### **Social Media Rules**

* **IMPAQ Curriculum**: The IMPAQ logo must be present in promotions; the curriculum must be mentioned in captions.

* **Watermarks**: Use the tertiary icon as a watermark at ~30% opacity in the bottom right corner.

* **Privacy**: Always obtain parental consent before posting pictures of children.

* **Interaction**: Respond to queries with warmth, kindness, and clarity.
